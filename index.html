About Future Paints

At Future Paints Limited, we manufacture and supply a wide range of paint products including Weather Guard paint, Under Coat paint, Silk Vinyl paint, Gloss paint, Emulsion paint, Texture paint, Road Marking paint, and many other decorative and protective coatings. In addition to paint manufacturing, we also provide professional painting services, color consultation, epoxy floor installation, POP ceiling installation, TV console installation, wallpaper installation, and complete interior finishing solutions.

Whether you are constructing a new building, renovating your home, upgrading office spaces, or handling a large commercial project, Future Paints Limited has the expertise, modern production capability, and professional team required to deliver superior paint products and finishing services tailored to your needs.

At Future Paints Limited, we believe quality paint is more than just color — it is protection, beauty, durability, and identity. We remain committed to manufacturing premium paint products, delivering exceptional workmanship, offering competitive pricing, and ensuring complete customer satisfaction in every project we undertake.

Our Interior Design Process

At Future Paints, we follow a collaborative and tailored approach to bring your interior design vision to life. Here’s an overview of our design process:

Consultation

  • Initial meeting to discuss your project goals, budget, and design style
  • Site assessment and measurement of the space

Design Concept

  • Development of a personalized design concept and mood board
  • Presentation of design ideas and revisions as needed

Space Planning

  • Creation of a detailed floor plan and 3D renderings
  • Furniture, fixture, and equipment (FF&E) selection and placement

Design Development

  • Refined design details, including lighting, color schemes, and textures
  • Material and finish selections

Implementation

  • Procurement of materials and furniture
  • Project management and coordination with contractors and vendors

Installation

  • On-site installation and supervision
  • Final walk-through and completion of punch list items

Final Touches

  • Final inspection and completion of any remaining tasks
  • Post-project evaluation and follow-up
